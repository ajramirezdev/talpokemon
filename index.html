HOW TO PLAY:

Defeat one of the Elite Five Chickens and become the Talpokemon Master

Move around the map using the arrow keys.

If you walk close enough to one of the Elite Five Chickens, they will challenge you into battle!

But don't worry you have your own chicken.

Use your mouse to click on the move you want your chicken to perform to fight back.
